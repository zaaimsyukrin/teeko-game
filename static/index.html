How to Play

Get 4 of your pieces in a row — horizontally, vertically, diagonally, or in a 2x2 square. Just like in tic-tac-toe!

Drop Phase: Take turns placing pieces until all 8 are on the board.

Move Phase: Move one piece to an adjacent square each turn.
